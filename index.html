Quality Assurance Analyst

Hi! I’m Joven Christian D. Santos, a Quality Assurance Analyst focused on helping web applications function smoothly and consistently. I approach systems by understanding how they’re built, analyzing how they behave, and ensuring that each feature meets its intended requirements. My background in web development supports my ability to evaluate functionality, user flows, and overall usability.

During my internship at Pixel8 Web Solutions & Consultancy Inc., I worked on documentation, user flows, UI mockups, and early QA validations for several internal systems. This experience strengthened my ability to analyze modules, create organized references for teams, and support clear, structured project workflows.

I maintain and expand my expertise through personal projects, applying test automation with Playwright and API testing with Postman. This ongoing practice supports a consistent and reliable foundation in software quality assurance.
